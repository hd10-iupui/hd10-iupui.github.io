## About Me

I am a researcher working at the intersection of language models, graph algorithms, and biomedical data science. My work focuses on combining Transformer-based semantic understanding with graph-based global relationship modeling for scientific discovery and healthcare applications.

My recent research includes graph-augmented keyphrase extraction, long-document understanding, biomedical relationship discovery, and interpretable machine learning for clinical and molecular data.
